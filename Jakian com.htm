Corporate Profile

公司願景 Our Vision

傑肯企業有限公司致力於成為全球五金工具市場的技術領航者。 Jakian Enterprises Co., Ltd. is dedicated to becoming a technological leader in the global hardware and DIY tools market.

卓越品質 Excellence in Quality

我們專注於研發高效能電動噴槍與專業塗裝工具，結合創新技術與卓越耐用性，滿足工業與專業人士的嚴苛要求。 Focusing on high-performance electric spray guns and professional coating tools, we combine innovation with durability to meet the rigorous demands of industrial professionals worldwide.

全球佈局 Global Footprint

憑藉數十年的製造經驗與嚴格的品質控管，傑肯的產品已行銷至世界各地，成為國際客戶信賴的供應夥伴。 With decades of manufacturing expertise and stringent quality control, Jakian's products are exported globally, making us a trusted partner for international clients.
